A little about me

I am a student at Texas A&M University in College Station, Texas, pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Industrial Distribution, along with a Business Minor, due for graduation in the spring of 2015. When I was 12, I dedicated my life to Jesus and seek to bring the integrity and wisdom of the Bible into practice daily.
I currently work in the Talent Incubator Program in the Global Supply Chain Lab at Texas A&M, doing undergraduate research for industry projects.
